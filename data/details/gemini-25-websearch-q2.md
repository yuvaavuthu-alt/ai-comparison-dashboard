### Comparing Your Purchase Options

    * **Promotions:** This is the *required* method to get any of Verizon's major trade-in deals.

3.  **Monthly Installments with Trade-In Promotion (Recommended):**
    * **How it works:** This is the option that provides the lowest device cost. You buy the new flagship phone (iPhone or Android) on the 36-month installment plan. You then trade in your eligible existing phone.

    * **Example:**
        * You buy the iPhone 17, which costs **$23.05/mo**.
        * You trade in an eligible phone and qualify for an **$830 promotion**.
        * Verizon applies a **$23.05 credit** to your bill each month for 36 months.
        * **Your net cost for the phone is $0/month.**
